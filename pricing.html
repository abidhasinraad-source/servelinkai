Can the AI Receptionist answer questions about my business?

Yes. Your AI Receptionist is trained using your business information, including your services, service areas, business hours, pricing (if applicable), and frequently asked questions. It can answer customer inquiries, capture leads, and collect appointment requests 24/7.
